# Electrodialysis Water Purification

Remove dissolved metals, salts, and minerals from well water or creek water using electricity and Rowow membranes.

## How It Works

Alternating cation (SAC) and anion (SBA) membranes with an electric field. Ions migrate through the membranes into concentrate channels, leaving purified water behind. Same Rowow painting technique, different resin types.

- Membranes never exhaust — electricity does the work
- Polarity reversal (EDR) self-cleans scale deposits weekly
- Charcoal-filled channels double as activated carbon filters
- Electric field regenerates the carbon filter — 5-10x longer life

## Drinking Water System

1. **Stage 1 — Electrodialysis:** Removes dissolved metals, salts, minerals (combined with carbon filtration)
2. **Stage 2 — UV sterilization:** $30-50 Amazon UV-C lamp, kills 99.9% bacteria/viruses

**Total cost: $100-150** vs $1,500-4,000 conventional well water treatment.

## Integration with Flow Battery

- The concentrate stream (2-3x mineral concentration) can be used for garden irrigation
- High-iron well water concentrate = additional battery electrolyte feedstock
- Powered by the flow battery during the day, solar at peak
