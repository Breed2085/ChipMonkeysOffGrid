# SEM Cell — Salt Electro Mining

Make hydrochloric acid and sodium hydroxide from table salt, water, and electricity.

Based on [Rowow's SEM TECH documentation](https://rowow.net).

## How It Works

Two containers separated by a cation exchange membrane (same Rowow membrane as the battery). Graphite electrodes. Saturated brine. 12V DC.

- **Anode** produces HCl (hydrochloric acid)
- **Cathode** produces NaOH (sodium hydroxide / lye)

## Fast Method

Load scrap iron directly into the anode chamber. The acid produced continuously dissolves iron, regenerating the acid and producing FeCl₂ electrolyte in one step.

## Safety

**CRITICAL: The anode produces CHLORINE GAS. Run this outdoors ONLY.**

- Chlorine gas is toxic — even small amounts irritate lungs
- Outdoors with breeze, upwind position
- If you smell "swimming pool," move away
- Hydrogen gas at cathode — no flames
